# Remote Work Syndicate — 30-Day Launch Plan v1.0

Recommended file path:

`/docs/08-launch-plan.md`

This document defines the first 30 days of execution for Remote Work Syndicate.

The goal of the first 30 days is not to build the perfect product.

The goal is to launch a credible, useful MVP that can generate:

- Early organic traffic
- Newsletter subscribers
- Apply/referral clicks
- Platform Match Quiz completions
- Community outcome reports
- Real market feedback

---

## 1. Launch Philosophy

Remote Work Syndicate should launch as soon as it is useful and trustworthy.

Do not wait for:

- perfect design
- every platform researched
- every job listed
- advanced automation
- user accounts
- admin dashboard
- paid features

Launch when the site can help a user:

1. Understand what Remote Work Syndicate is.
2. Compare major AI work platforms.
3. Browse verified opportunities.
4. Take the Platform Match Quiz.
5. Subscribe for updates.
6. Apply through a tracked/referral link.
7. Share an application or platform outcome.

---

## 11. Launch Risks

### Risk 1: Too much building, not enough distribution

Mitigation:

Post daily. Talk to users daily. Publish content weekly.

### Risk 2: Generic job board positioning

Mitigation:

Lead with platform intelligence, comparison, and quiz.

### Risk 3: Referral programs change or reject public promotion

Mitigation:

Do not depend on only one platform. Keep trust/inventory platforms for SEO. Add sponsorships and services later.

### Risk 4: Thin SEO pages

Mitigation:

Do not index pages without unique value.

### Risk 5: Fake or low-quality community reports

Mitigation:

Use verification levels and publish only aggregated insights.

### Risk 6: Overdesigned MVP

Mitigation:

Use premium UI, but keep scope tight.
